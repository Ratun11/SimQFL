What Is Quantum Federated Learning (QFL)?

Quantum Federated Learning is an emerging discipline combining the distributed power of federated learning with the computational advantages of quantum computing. By training models across decentralized data sources while leveraging quantum-enhanced algorithms, QFL opens up new possibilities in speed, security, and overall performance.

  • Data Privacy: Federated learning ensures data stays where it is generated, reducing security risks.
  • Quantum Acceleration: Quantum algorithms may solve complex problems faster than classical counterparts.
  • Scalability: Ideal for large-scale collaborative projects across multiple sites or devices.
